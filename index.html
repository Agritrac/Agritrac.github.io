Benjamin's custom retro lunchboxes 



My name is Benjamin and I am 10 years old. Dad and me searched the shops to find a lunch box, but all we could find was lunch bags that squashed my sandwiches or tiny boxes that I could not fit my banana in. 
So  Dad and me found these retro lunch boxes.

Hygienic easy clean plastic 

Our lunch boxes are made from responsibly sourced hard wearing plastic. They are easy to clean and disinfect and are perfect for the current times, where we all have to take extra steps to protect each other.

The plastic will not absorb any bacteria or viruses.  So any green sandwiches can be cleaned out with no staining or lingering smell.
